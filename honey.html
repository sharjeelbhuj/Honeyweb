HONEY ON A ROYAL MISSION

The smoothest, richest, and creamiest honey on earth.
Each jar is on a mission to support regenerative farming so we can holistically
restore the health of people and beauty to our planet.

MORE THAN JUST DELICIOUS FOOD

When you spread the natural goodness of Crème Royale on bread, pastries, pancakes, rice cakes or any of your
favorite foods, you’ll know you’re also helping spread the word about the benefits and importance of regenerative
farming to ensure an ecologically sustainable future for all.
